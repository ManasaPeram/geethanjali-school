Welcome to Geethanjali Talent School

A Place Where Learning Comes Alive

Established in 2003, Geethanjali Talent School is committed to nurturing young minds from Daycare through to Class 10, providing a comprehensive educational experience that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility.

Our mission is to create an environment that serves as the groundwork for a future filled with opportunities and a starting point for individuals' aspirations. Through our efforts, we strive to inspire, educate, and enlighten every person to become agents of "Leadership for a Better World".

About Us

Building a Legacy of Excellence

Founded by Mr. P. Srinivasa Rao, a visionary with over two decades of experience in education, Geethanjali Talent School has grown from a modest beginning into a respected institution. His commitment to quality education and innovative teaching methods has been instrumental in our success.

Our school is affiliated with the Board of Secondary Education, Telangana (BSE Telangana), offering a robust academic program from Class 1 to Class 10. We provide modern facilities including smart classrooms, sports facilities, library resources, and safe transportation.

Our Philosophy

  • Holistic Education: Equal emphasis on academics and co-curricular activities
  • Technology-Integrated Learning: Modern teaching strategies and tools
  • Individual Attention: Personalized support for each student
  • Environmental Awareness: Promoting sustainable green initiatives
  • Global Perspectives: Cultivating a diverse and inclusive community

Our Vision

To create an environment that nurtures future leaders through comprehensive education, focusing on academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility while preparing students for global challenges.

Arts & Crafts

Sports

Music

Drama Club

Build confidence and communication skills through acting, script reading, and stage performances.

Computer Lab

Learn essential computer skills, coding basics, and digital literacy in our well-equipped computer lab.

Yoga

Improve mental focus, flexibility, and well-being through regular yoga sessions for all age groups.

Gardening Club

Festival-wise Activities
